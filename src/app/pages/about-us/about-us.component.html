About us

Creative Hub is a transformational hub focused on providing individuals the core elements of success: job-ready skills and well being.

We are providing a combination of real-world skills and workout sessions for aspiring professionals in the areas of front-end development, digital marketing, graphic design, web design, data science, e-commerce, etc.

Our success is based on the individual-tailored help that we give to each student upon graduation of the bootcamp.

Our team is fully committed not only to provide you with skills but through our hiring & learning process to match you with internship opportunities or jobs.

Our mission is to help 1 000 000 individuals to improve their life, by gaining the right skills, being in the right body-mind balance, and find the career that will never feel like a job.
