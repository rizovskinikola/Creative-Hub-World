Program Requirements

Admissions Requirements

Joining Creative Hub as a Bootcamp student requires you to meet the minimum requirements outlined below:

  1. Have a high school diploma or equivalent, or
  2. Be at least 18 years of age and pass the Wonderlic Basic Skills Test (Level 3) with minimum scores of 303 for verbal and 294 for quantitative.

    3. In addition to:

  3. Satisfactory completion of a Personal Statement.
  4. Satisfactory completion of an Admissions Interview.
  5. Satisfactory completion of a Technical Challenge.
  6. If English is a second language, completion and submission of one of the following:
    1. Minimum overall score of 90 on the TOEFL iBT tes
    2. Minimum overall score of 90 on the TOEFL iBT tes

    7. Contact our Admissions Team if you have any questions about these requirements.

Technology Requirements

Required course materials and technological resources not provided by the institution:

  • Students are required to bring their own laptop to the program (Mac or Windows, not Chromebook):
    • CPU: Intel i5 - i7 processor with at least 2.7 GHz or M1 processor
    • Minimum 128 GB HDD/SSD (recommended 256GB)
    • Minimum 8GB RAM (recommended 16GB)
    • Mac 0S v10.13 High Sierra or Windows 10
  • Students also need a webcam and microphone.
  • In addition to these requirements, students must ensure that their system meets the requirements of the videoconferencing tool, Zoom outlined here
  • It is preferred that students use the latest version of Chrome as their browser
