PROGRAM

Go to market Strategy

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Understand all the elements that you need to know prior to launching a digital marketing strategy on a market
  • Recognize the process of Go-to-market and set the plan
  • Create your own checklist that will help you in future when you launch a go-to-market strategy

Buyer Persona

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Grasp how to create buyer personas for your business
  • Learn to create a value proposition for the buyer persona of your choice
  • Master converting value proposition into an ad copy

Ad Content Creation

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Know strategies on content writing for ads
  • Understand the process of storytelling
  • Work on your own copy using practices and create high quality content strategy
  • Design different ad copy for different stages at the sales cycle, leads, prospects and customers

Facebook Advertising

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Create convertible ads using the Fb ads manager
  • Optimize and test the ads
  • Measure the performance of ads in Facebook
  • Memorize the different stages upon ad creation
  • Target & retarget audience strategies
  • Perform with different ad sets and ads

Embedding Digital Marketing Strategy in UX UI Design

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Attend a visiting class with a UX UI Designer and Front-end Developer as part of the program where you will work together and understand the role of the Digital Marketer in the process flow
  • Suggest lead magnets and convertible content while working on design mockups with the team

Retargeting Strategies

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Connect different elements in the Digital Marketing Strategy such as Facebook Ads, Website & Email Marketing to create re-targeting strategies that will convert prospects into buyers
  • Work and Practice on a real-project and conversions

Email Marketing

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Create Email Marketing Strategies for a client and execute them
  • Embed the Email Marketing as part of the overall digital marketing strategy
  • Deep dive into the subject and automate the processes, using Email Marketing Software
  • Create lead magnets, website forms, pop ups, using Email Marketing Software and embed them on a website
  • Measure if your Email Marketing Strategy is converting leads into clients
  • all students will be given free access during the classes to Active Campaign Email Marketing Software

Digital Marketing KPI’s

After Completing this bootcamp, you will be able to:
  • Analyze your own performance by setting up the right KPIs for you
  • Draft reports for clients based on conversion data from your work
