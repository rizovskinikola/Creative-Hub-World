Qarterly Report for Production Plan

Using the knowledge of the subject of Power BI & SQL, the students have created a report of their choice with country specifications, sales agent, product, and production plant.

The report used data for the cost of goods sold, revenues and profits.

Additionally, they have created visuals for a cart cost of goods sold by country, sales unit by country, total sale by segment, etc.

Strategic Report for Unemployment Rate Q1 2021

The students had a task to make a report analyzing a large set of data according to the number of unemployed by industries and index.

Using functions they have sorted the data and produced a visual and automated analysis that can be used for strategic purposes.
