Who is this for?

This course is for anybody seeking to alter their career and begin a new one in the field of graphic design.

Whether you have prior experience or not, you'll be given 40 weeks to master all there is to know about design and create an original portfolio.

What DO you need?

A burning desire to get started and passion for graphic design.

A goal plan that you will want to achieve for yourself in the following year, with a committed partner like us by your side.
